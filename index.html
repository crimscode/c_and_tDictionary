WELCOME TO THE c&t: Dictionary

:EMOJI MEANINGS:

:salad:
SALUTE

MEANING:

Being the very first emoji with a meaning, the emoji :salad: means SALUTE; originating from the 'sa' in :salad:.

:seedling:
CYA

MEANING:

The emoji :seedling: is a very unique emoji with a unique meaning behind it too. Despite the meaning of :seedling: being 'CYA', the origin of it comes from the part 'see' (see ya/see ya later).

:trolleybus:
TROLL

MEANING:

The :trolleybus: is indeed a funny emoji, and coincidentally has the full meaning of it in the emoji itself. As you may already have seen, the meaning of :trolleybus: is 'TROLL', coming from 'troll' in :trolleybus:.

:hibiscus:
IS COOL

MEANING:

This emoji is pretty unused since the dawn of this language, but it is pretty cool. The meaning 'IS COOL' comes from the 'is' in :hibiscus:.

:handshake:
AGREEMENT

MEANING:

The handshake emoji is the firm, trusting, manly handshake that thanks, greets friends and makes friendships, and best of all, thanks other fellow men and people. :handshake: means everything related to 'THANKING'.

:flag_no:
NO PROBLEM

MEANING:

Flags actually have a lot of meanings in this very particular language. This flag, :flag_no:, means 'NO PROBLEM', coming from the part 'no' in :flag_no: which is actually pretty fortunate and nice.
